About FR Designs

Rishan Khan - Founder

Rishan Khan is a civil engineer and interior designer based in Goa. He is the founder of THE FR DESIGN STUDIO, a firm specializing in merging creativity with practical execution in architectural and interior design. The studio has successfully completed over 50 projects across various Goan locations like Panjim, Porvorim, Margao, Sanklim, and Bicholim. The FR Design Studio is a brand that goes beyond interior design and takes on a unique and creative approach to every single project with a deep understanding of its purpose, and the people using it. The studio works closely with clients to deliver timeless design and spaces with impeccable style and sophistication.
